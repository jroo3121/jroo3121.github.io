Banana Bread

Ingredients:

4 overripe bananas

3/4 cup sugar

1 tsp baking soda

1 tsp salt

1 egg

1/4 cup oil

Chocolate chips (make it as chocolatey as you want. I usually use 2/3 cup)

1.5 cups flour

Directions:

1. Preheat oven to 325 Fahrenheit 2. Mash the bananas on a plate with a fork until there are no more solid chunks. 3. Put the bananas in a mixing bowl 4. Add sugar, soda and salt. 5. Add egg and oil, then mix 6. Add flour and chocolate chips, then mix 7. Grease Muffins 350 degrees 26 minutes Loaf pan 325 degrees 1 hour
